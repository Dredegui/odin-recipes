Fish and Chips

Description

Fish and Chips is a classic dish of the British Isles, consisting of battered and deep-fried fish, usually cod or haddock, and french fries.

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 35 mins
  • Rest Time: 30 mins
  • Total: 85 mins
  • Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 7 tablespoons (55 grams) all-purpose flour, divided
  • 7 tablespoons (55 grams) cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Sea salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/3 cup dark beer, cold
  • 1/3 cup sparkling water, cold
  • 4 fish fillets
  • 2 pounds potatoes, peeled
  • 1 quart (1 liter) vegetable oil , or lard, for frying

Steps

  1. Mix flour with cornstarch and baking powder
  2. Season the mix with salt and pepper
  3. Whisk the mix while adding the beer and the sparkling water
  4. Place the batter in the fridge to rest for 30 min to 1h
  5. Cut potatoes
  6. Fry potatoes
  7. Season the fillets
  8. Add flour to the fillets
  9. Dip into the batter
  10. Fry at 350F for 8 min
